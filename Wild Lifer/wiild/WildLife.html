EAGLE
Eagles are large, powerful birds of prey known for their sharp eyesight, strong talons, and impressive wingspan. They are found on every continent except Antarctica, inhabiting a variety of habitats, including forests, mountains, and coastlines. These majestic birds are apex predators, playing a crucial role in maintaining the balance of their ecosystems.

OWL
Owls are fascinating nocturnal birds of prey known for their silent flight, keen eyesight, and distinctive hooting calls. They are found on every continent except Antarctica, inhabiting a variety of habitats, from forests to deserts and grasslands.
DESCRIPTION
CROW
Crows are intelligent and adaptable birds known for their black plumage, distinctive cawing calls, and problem-solving abilities. They are found in a wide variety of habitats across the globe, from urban areas to forests and grasslands.

BUTTERFLY
Butterflies are insects with large, often brightly colored wings. They are known for their metamorphosis, a process in which they transform from a caterpillar to a pupa and finally to a winged adult. Butterflies are found in a wide variety of habitats, from tropical rainforests to meadows and gardens. They play an important role in the ecosystem as pollinators, helping to fertilize plants.

KINGFISHER
Kingfishers are small, brightly colored birds known for their striking appearance and their ability to dive into water to catch fish. They are found near bodies of water around the world, inhabiting a variety of habitats, from rivers and streams to lakes and coastlines.

PARROT
Parrots are a diverse group of birds known for their vibrant colors, strong beaks, and remarkable ability to mimic human speech. They are found in tropical and subtropical regions around the world, inhabiting a variety of habitats, including forests, grasslands, and coastal areas.

HERON
Herons are long-legged wading birds with long necks and sharp beaks. They are found in wetlands, marshes, and along shorelines worldwide. These graceful birds are skilled hunters, using their long necks and sharp beaks to spear fish and other aquatic prey.
